Every month a new journey

Enter the world of exciting literary adventure with our exclusive book subscription service! Each month, we offer you the opportunity to choose a book from a wide selection of genres: from adventure to contemporary fiction, from nonfiction to science fiction and much more.
Forget the endless search in the bookshop: thanks to us, you will save precious time, avoid queues and the stress of choosing. With the convenience of home delivery, each book becomes a unique and surprising experience.

Whether you're an avid reader or want to explore new genres, there's a subscription plan that's perfect for you.

We offer flexible subscription plans that fit your reading style. Our service allows you to choose between different subscription options. If you want to enjoy each page calmly, our 'Serial reader' plan guarantees you a literary surprise every thirty days. For those hungry for stories, the 'Bibliophile' plan offers a double dose of adventure to experience. And for true book lovers, the 'Bibliomaniac' plan ensures you have a constant flow of new stories to explore.

With a single subscription, you'll have access to a variety of books designed to engage every member of the family: from colorful children's stories to engaging fiction (and more!) for adults.

Genres

Contemporary Fiction

Discover the most powerful voices of our time! Get lost in the pages of authors who are shaping the modern literary landscape: from the narrative mastery of Jonathan Franzen and Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie, to the compelling plots of Donna Tartt and Richard Powers. Enter the world of contemporary fiction and be enchanted by the stories that define our present.

Classic literature

From timeless masterpieces to iconic works, our collection will take you on exciting journeys across eras and cultures. From F. Scott Fitzgerald to Lev Tolstoy, from Charles Dickens to Jane Austen, we have a vast selection of the greatest authors of all time. these classics are the fabric of human culture. By reading them, you will immerse yourself in ethical debates, profound reflections and unforgettable adventures.

Romance

Enter stories that make your heart beat and captivate you from the first page. Our romance book series takes you on a journey through the power of love, friendship, and deep emotions. From first loves to mature loves, explore the vast panorama of human emotions through the pages of our sentimental books. Through the pages of authors such as Nicholas Sparks and Jojo Moyes, discover the power of love that transforms lives and enchants readers!

Fantasy

Enter a universe of magic, adventure and mystery with our collection of fantasy books, starring George R.R. Martin, J.K. Rowling, J.R.R. Tolkien and mony more. From epic battles to journeys through enchanted lands, from dragon lands to elven cities, from majestic dragons to cunning goblins, these books will take you to enchanted and adventurous places. From epic fantasy to timeless tales of magic, be transported on extraordinary journeys that challenge the imagination.

Mystery

Enter the world of intrigue and suspense, from unsolved puzzles to unexpected twists, each book is a journey into the heart of the investigation. From Agatha Christie to Arthur Conan Doyle, from Gillian Flynn to Raymond Chandler, every story is a guarantee of engaging suspense. Guess the murderer, discover the deception or decipher the enigma together with the protagonists, in an engaging game of deductions.

Science fiction

Get ready to travel beyond the boundaries of time and space! From remote galaxies to dystopian futures, each book is an immersion in alternative worlds and surprising technologies. With authors such as Isaac Asimov, Philip K. Dick, Ursula K. Le Guin, and Arthur C. Clarke, travel to unknown planets, explore alien civilizations, and experience new frontiers of technology and society. From perfect futuristic societies to apocalyptic futures, each story offers a unique perspective on the fate of humanity.

Horror

Step into the darkness with our collection of horror books. From knocks on the door at night to sinister presences, each book is a journey into the unknown, ready to make your skin crawl. From Stephen King to H.P. Lovecraft, from Edgar Allan Poe to Shirley Jackson, each book is a descent into the darkest recesses of the mind. Spooky creatures, eerie ghosts and monsters of the darkness, these novels will take you to disturbing worlds ready to test your courage

Children

Give your little readers a passport to the imagination! each book is an invitation to explore, learn and enjoy, featuring authors such as Roald Dahl, Dr. Seuss and Enid Blyton. Engaging stories that teach important values: friendship, courage and acceptance of differences. Children's books are a treasure that stimulates creativity and a love of reading from an early age.

Theatre

From classic drama to brilliant comedies, each book is a window into emotion and the power of spoken words. William Shakespeare, Anton Chekhov, Tennessee Williams, Oscar Wilde: immerse yourself in vibrant dialogues and unforgettable monologues that bring the stage right into your hands. These books are a treasure for those who love the art of acting and the emotions experienced through words.

Poetry

Tune into the melodies of the words with the verses of Emily Dickinson, Pablo Neruda, Maya Angelou, Walt Whitman and many more. Each line contains the beauty and depth of emotion in enchanting verses. Poets' words can be a refuge, a guide, or a celebration of life's joys and struggles. Treat yourself to a journey into the infinity of emotions and the beauty of words.

Graphic novels

Stan Lee, Alan Moore, Frank Miller, Neil Gaiman, just to name a few, will open your mind with their detailed drawings and breathtaking art, in an explosion of creativity and imagination. From legendary superheroes to intimate and complex stories, the world of graphic novels offers endless, colorful adventures to experience page after page.

Plans

Each plan is designed to adapt to the different needs of readers, offering a unique experience and enhancing the passion for reading with exclusive benefits and opportunities for involvement in the literary community.
